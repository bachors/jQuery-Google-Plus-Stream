jQuery Google Plus-Stream

View on GitHub

jQuery Google Plus Stream is jQuery plugin to display your google+ timeline steams (posts, photos, videos, article) on your website.

Sample

<!-- HTML -->
<div id="GPstream"></div>

<!-- JS -->
<script>
GPstream({
    user: '+username or userID',
    key: 'API KEY'
});
</script>
